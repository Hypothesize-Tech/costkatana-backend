# ROI & Benchmarking

## What It Is

Cost Katana helps you estimate the return on investment (ROI) of AI cost optimization. You can see projected savings from adopting Cost Katana, compare different scenarios, and track how your optimizations perform over time.

## What You Get

- **ROI calculator** – Enter your current AI spend, team size, and use cases to see projected savings
- **Multiple scenarios** – Conservative, moderate, and optimistic projections
- **Industry benchmarks** – Benchmarks from case studies and industry data
- **Cost simulation** – Estimate request cost before running (used by the gateway for budget checks)
- **Experiment tracking** – Run A/B tests (e.g., different models or prompts) and compare results
- **Savings tracking** – See actual savings from applied optimizations

## How to Use the ROI Calculator

You provide:

- Use cases (e.g., customer support, code generation)
- Current AI spend
- Team size and hours spent on AI tasks
- Company size and industry
- Implementation budget and time horizon

Cost Katana returns projected labor savings, cost avoidance, and productivity gains across conservative, moderate, and optimistic scenarios.

## FAQ

**How accurate are the projections?**  
Benchmarks come from case studies and industry data. Scenarios help you see a range rather than a single number.

**Can I run experiments?**  
Yes. You can compare templates, models, or configurations and see which performs better on cost and quality.

**Where do I see my actual savings?**  
In the dashboard analytics. Cost Katana tracks optimizations you apply and reports real savings.
