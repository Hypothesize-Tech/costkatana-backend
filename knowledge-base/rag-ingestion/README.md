# How Cost Katana's Chat Finds Answers

## What You Get

When you ask Cost Katana's chat about features, setup, or troubleshooting, it uses an intelligent search system to find the most relevant information from our documentation, your usage data, and ingested content—then generates clear, accurate answers.

## How It Works (Simple)

1. **Your Question** → Cost Katana understands what you're asking
2. **Smart Search** → It searches across documentation, code, and your data using both meaning and keywords
3. **Best Results** → Results are ranked by relevance, so you see the most helpful content first
4. **Answer** → The AI uses those results to give you a tailored answer

## What Gets Searched

- **Platform Docs** – Feature guides, integrations, best practices
- **Your Usage & Telemetry** – Historical data, patterns, trends
- **Code Repos** (if connected) – Your GitHub code for context-aware help
- **Your Chats** – Past conversations for continuity
- **Uploaded Files** – Documents you've added for analysis

## Semantic Caching: Save on Repeat Questions

If you ask something similar to a previous question, Cost Katana can reuse a cached answer instead of calling the AI again. This saves ~70–80% of cost on repeat queries and speeds up responses.

## FAQ

**Why does the chat sometimes take a moment to answer?**  
It's searching across many sources and ranking results to give you the best answer. More complex questions take slightly longer.

**Can it search my private data?**  
Yes, if you connect integrations (e.g., GitHub) or upload documents. Search is scoped to your account.

**Why do answers sometimes cite documentation?**  
Answers are grounded in our docs and your data so they stay accurate and up to date.
