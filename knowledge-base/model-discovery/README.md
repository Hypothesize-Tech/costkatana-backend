# Model Discovery & Intelligent Routing

## What It Is

Cost Katana knows about 400+ AI models across 10+ providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, AWS Bedrock, and more). It helps you find the right model for each use case, get suggestions to save cost, and route requests intelligently.

## What You Get

- **Model catalog** – Browse models by provider, capability (chat, embedding, vision), and cost
- **Proactive suggestions** – Cost Katana recommends cheaper or better models based on your usage
- **Intelligent routing** – Requests can be routed by capability, cost, or latency
- **Optimization templates** – Pre-built templates for common optimization patterns
- **Benchmarks** – Anonymous benchmarks from other users (privacy-preserving) to compare performance

## Proactive Suggestions

Cost Katana analyzes your usage and suggests changes such as:

- Switching to a cheaper model for simple tasks
- Using a more capable model when quality matters
- Adjusting parameters to reduce cost

You can accept or reject suggestions. Your feedback helps improve future recommendations.

## FAQ

**How many models does Cost Katana support?**  
400+ models across 10+ providers, including OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, AWS Bedrock, and others.

**How do I get model suggestions?**  
Suggestions appear in the dashboard and in chat when Cost Katana spots optimization opportunities.

**Can I force a specific model?**  
Yes. You can override the model in your request or gateway context. Intelligent routing is optional.
