# Frequently Asked Questions for Cost Katana Users

Common questions users ask about Cost Katana features, setup, and usage. Use this for quick answers in chat.

## Getting Started

**What is Cost Katana?**  
Cost Katana is an AI cost optimization platform. It helps you track, analyze, and reduce your AI API costs across multiple providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, AWS Bedrock, etc.).

**How do I get started?**  
Sign up at costkatana.com, create an account, and connect your first project. You can use the CLI, SDK, or dashboard to start tracking usage.

**What can I track?**  
You can track AI requests: models used, tokens, cost, latency. You can break this down by project, workspace, or time period.

## Billing & Plans

**What plans does Cost Katana offer?**  
Free, Plus, Pro, and Enterprise. Plans differ by features, usage limits, and support.

**How do I upgrade my plan?**  
Go to the dashboard → Billing → Upgrade. Add a payment method if needed, then choose your plan.

**What happens if I exceed my budget?**  
Cost Katana can block new requests when you hit your budget. You’ll get alerts as you approach the limit. Increase your budget or wait for the next period to continue.

## Integrations

**What can I connect to Cost Katana?**  
GitHub, Vercel, MongoDB, AWS, Google, Slack, Jira, Linear, Discord, ChatGPT, and Cursor. Each integration has its own setup flow.

**How do I connect my GitHub?**  
Go to Integrations → GitHub → Connect. Authorize with GitHub, then choose which repos to link.

**Can I use Cost Katana with Cursor?**  
Yes. Install the Cost Katana Cursor extension. It will track your AI usage from Cursor and give you cost insights.

## Cost & Optimization

**How does Cost Katana reduce my costs?**  
Through Cortex (40–75% token reduction), model suggestions, prompt optimization, semantic caching, and budgeting controls.

**What is Cortex?**  
Cortex is Cost Katana’s optimization system. It compresses AI output so you use fewer tokens while keeping quality. It can cut costs by 40–75% on supported flows.

**How do I ask about my costs in plain English?**  
Use CKQL. Ask questions like "What were my total AI costs last month?" or "Show top projects by cost" and get structured answers.

## AWS & Governance

**Can I manage AWS with natural language?**  
Yes. With the AWS integration, you can ask to stop EC2 instances, start RDS databases, and more. Cost Katana creates a plan, you approve it, and it runs securely.

**Is it safe?**  
Yes. Every execution needs your approval. You can use the kill switch to stop everything. Cost Katana uses your IAM role with minimal permissions.

**What are guardrails?**  
Guardrails let you limit what AI agents can do: which models they use, budget caps, and which actions need approval.

## Teams & Support

**How do I invite my team?**  
Go to Team settings → Invite. Enter emails; teammates get an invitation to join.

**Where do I get help?**  
Use the in-app chat for support, check the documentation, or use live chat when available.
